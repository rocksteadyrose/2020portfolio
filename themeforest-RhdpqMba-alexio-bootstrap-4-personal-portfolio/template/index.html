I'm Leslie Rose

I'm a children's media content consultant with proven success in creating specialized and unique digital material for kids + a full-stack web developer who’s especially passionate about the front end of kids’ applications.

Social Media Specialist

I've created, published, and moderated social content across over twenty-five social accounts for a top children's media brand, strengthened brand awareness and engagement through partner posts, and organically increased this brand's fan base by over 127% in less than 6 months through unique social strategies.

Programming Specialist

Through specialized strategies, outreach, customer support, presentations, and/or curricular advisement, I've helped support successful programming, strengthen brand relationships, and develop new programs.

Web Developer

Through an intensive 24-week long boot camp, I built web apps and gained skills in HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, JQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars.js, and ReactJS. I most recently put these skills to work in my role as a Club Success Specialist at Girls Who Code.

Puppeteer

Creator
